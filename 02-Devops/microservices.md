# What is Microservice?

a collection of smaller services/processes

- Inventory Service (10 replicas)
- User Service (2 replicas)
- Cart Service (3 replicas)
- Notification Service (20 replicas)
- Shipping Service (10 replicas)
- Load Balancer - Nginx
